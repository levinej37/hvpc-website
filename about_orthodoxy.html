About Orthodoxy

A library of articles on the Orthodox Christian faith

The Orthodox Church is the ancient, undivided Church founded by Christ and His Apostles, faithfully preserving the fullness of the Christian faith through Holy Scripture and Holy Tradition. She traces an unbroken succession from the day of Pentecost to the present day.

These articles are offered as an introduction to Orthodox Christian theology, worship, history, and practice. Whether you are a lifelong Orthodox Christian deepening your understanding, or a seeker encountering Orthodoxy for the first time, we hope these resources prove illuminating.

“Orthodoxy is not a denomination among many — it is the original Christianity, unchanged in its essential faith, worship, and life.”
