Written Directions

From Major Routes

▲ Driving from the North ▼

Via New York State Thruway (I-87) Southbound

  • 1Take the New York State Thruway (I-87 Southbound) to Exit 11 (Nyack).
  • 2Turn left onto Route 59 East.
  • 3Proceed to Route 9W (1st traffic light — approx. 0.3 mile). Turn right (South).
  • 4Proceed one block to Depew Avenue. Turn left (East).
  • 5Proceed to Anna Street (2nd street). Turn right (South).
  • 6Continue until you reach Cedarhill Avenue. Turn left (East).
  • 7Drive 6 blocks to the intersection with Prospect Street (approx. 0.25 mile). The Church will be on your left.
▶ Driving from New Jersey ▼

Via Garden State Parkway & New York State Thruway

Drive Northbound on the Garden State Parkway (GSP). Connect with the NY State Thruway Eastbound toward the Tappan Zee Bridge (Mario Cuomo Bridge). Continue on the Thruway until Exit 11 (Nyack).

From Exit 11, follow the directions outlined above under “Driving from the North.”
▶ From Westchester County — over the Tappan Zee Bridge ▼

Via New York State Thruway (I-87) Northbound

  • 1Take the NY State Thruway Northbound and take Exit 11 for Nyack.
  • 2Drive on High Avenue to the 1st traffic light (one block). Make a right onto Route 9W.
  • 3Continue until you reach Depew Avenue (3 blocks). Make a right.
From Depew Avenue, continue as indicated in the “Driving from the North” directions above (steps 5–7).
