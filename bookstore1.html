Daily Readings During Great Lent

«Чтение на каждый день Великого Поста»

$8.00

A wonderful, spiritually uplifting 447-page book whose aim is to give the reader a better understanding about the meaning of Great Lent. The passages and selected readings are noted for each day of the Great Lenten period, underscoring the significance of each day.

Each daily reading contains Holy Father’s teachings, explanations, essays and homilies associated with that particular day. The book also contains readings for the Great Lent Sundays as well as the Feast Days that are incorporated into the Great Lenten Season.

The Orthodox Study Bible

$50.00  Credit cards accepted

The Orthodox Study Bible is the fruit of over twenty years of labor by many of the best Orthodox Christian theologians of our time. This long-awaited single volume brings together an original translation of the Old Testament from the Septuagint with the classic Orthodox Study Bible: New Testament and Psalms.

The first-ever full-length Orthodox Study Bible in English presents the Bible of the early church. It uses the New King James Version as the basis for a fresh translation of the Septuagint text — the Greek version of the Bible used by Christ, the Apostles, and the early church.

New Testament from the New King James Version Commentary drawn from the early Church Fathers Easy-to-locate liturgical readings Book introductions and outlines Full-color icons and maps Psalms retranslated from the Septuagint Expanded notes, glossary and study articles
