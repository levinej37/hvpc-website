Our Parish

A Spiritual Home
in the Hudson Valley

Holy Virgin Protection Russian Orthodox Church in Nyack, New York has been a home for the faithful for generations. We are a parish of the Russian Orthodox Church Outside Russia (ROCOR), a canonical jurisdiction of the Moscow Patriarchate, celebrating the ancient and unchanged faith of the Apostles.

"We can either choose to be their neighbors like the Good Samaritan, or we can choose to close our eyes and walk away."

Divine services are celebrated in both English and Church Slavonic. All visitors and inquirers are warmly welcomed — whether lifelong Orthodox Christians, seekers, or those simply curious about the ancient faith.

About Our Jurisdiction

Russian Orthodox Church Outside Russia

ROCOR Official Site Holy Trinity Monastery Holy Trinity Seminary Mission in Jerusalem

The Russian Orthodox Church Outside Russia (ROCOR) is a semi-autonomous jurisdiction of the Moscow Patriarchate, originally formed in response to Bolshevik persecution following the Russian Revolution. ROCOR has over 400 parishes and monasteries in 40 countries worldwide, served by nearly 600 priests. In North America there are approximately 133 US parishes and 22 in Canada. The most important North American monastery is Holy Trinity Monastery in Jordanville, New York, home to the ROCOR seminary.

Church Interior & Grounds

Icons, Frescoes
& the Lives of Saints

The vaulted ceiling of our nave bears 24 icons of the saints, predominantly from the Old Testament. Upon entry, six wall icons of Russian Clerical Saints — lit by lampadas — greet the faithful on both sides of the nave.

From the Archangels Michael, Gabriel, and Raphael, to the holy Prophets and Forefathers of Christ — our church interior is a window into the full treasury of Orthodox iconographic tradition.

Archangel Gabriel

Messenger of God, who announced the births of John the Baptist and Jesus Christ to Zacharias and the Theotokos.

Archangel Michael

Archistrategos — Supreme Commander of the Heavenly Hosts, leader of God’s armies against the forces of darkness.
