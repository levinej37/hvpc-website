Application for Membership

Join the Parish of Holy Virgin Protection Russian Orthodox Church

We welcome all Orthodox Christians who wish to become members of our parish. Membership in the parish is open to all baptized Orthodox Christians of both sexes who have reached the age of 21, who make their confession and receive Holy Communion not less than once a year, and who desire to participate in the spiritual and material life of the community.

To apply, simply download the membership application form, complete it, and email the completed form to the Parish Rector.

1

Download the Application

Download the PDF application form using the button below. You can fill it out on your computer or print it and complete it by hand.

2

Complete All Sections

Fill in your personal information, family information, sacramental history, and contact details. Read the declaration at the bottom carefully before signing.

3

Email to Father Elias Gorsky

Email your completed application to the Parish Rector, Very Reverend Elias Gorsky, at frilya@egcon.com. You may also bring a printed copy to the church office.

4

Parish Council Review

Your application will be presented for acceptance at a meeting of the Parish Council and approved by the Rector's signature. You will be notified once your membership has been confirmed.
